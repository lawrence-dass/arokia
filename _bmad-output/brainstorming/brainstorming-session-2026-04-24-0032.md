# Brainstorming Session Results — Project Arokia

**Facilitator:** Lawrence
**Date:** 2026-04-24

## Session Overview

**Project:** **Arokia** (ஆரோக்கியம்) — Tamil-first Christian meditation & spiritual wellness mobile app.

**Name Meaning:** "Health, well-being, soundness." Tamil name rooted in Sanskrit Arogya ("without disease"). Personally significant (Lawrence's mother Arokia and uncle Arokia Dass). Tamil Catholic heritage as Arokia Matha (Our Lady of Good Health, Velankanni). Naturally pan-Indian due to shared Sanskrit root — supports multi-language roadmap.

### Three Mission Pillars

1. **🌍 Spread the Good News** — Deliver the words of Jesus in Tamil first, then Hindi, Telugu, and other Indian languages. Fill a gap in vernacular Christian digital content.

2. **🧠💪🕊️ Mind-Body-Soul Wholeness** — Every Christian is called to wholeness. Arokia helps with: **Soul** (follow Jesus's words, fulfill the aim of the soul), **Mind** (stay healthy through life's struggles — don't be negative), **Body** (take care of oneself to serve others and live long). For all Christians, not a single demographic.

3. **🛡️ Return to the Roots** — The world is noisy with false leaders, false preachers, misinformation, and showmanship Christianity. Arokia refuses that. It returns users to the authentic words of Jesus — plainly, without noise. Jeremiah 6:16, Matthew 7:15. Anti-drift, pro-authenticity.

### Core Principles (Non-Negotiable)

- ✅ **Strictly Christian** — New Testament, especially words of Jesus. No syncretism.
- ✅ **Tool, not Jesus** — The app never impersonates or speaks FOR Jesus. It is a means of encountering His words. Blasphemy avoidance is a UX requirement.
- ✅ **No Paraphrasing** — Scripture quoted verbatim. Commentary clearly labeled as human commentary.
- ✅ **Tamil first, Indian-language roadmap** — Hindi, Telugu, and beyond.
- ✅ **Donation-funded, no hard paywall** — Transparent crowdsourcing for servers/AI/dev costs. Optional "tithe" model.
- ✅ **Community-contributable** — Young Christians, pastors, theologians, musicians can help shape the app.
- ❌ **Not a Stoicism blend** — stoicism was only mentioned as a mindfulness reference; not part of the product philosophy.
- ❌ **No false-teaching amplification** — No celebrity pastors, no prosperity gospel, no engagement-farming.

## Phase 2 — Mind Mapping (REVISED Architecture)

### Key Architectural Insight (from Lawrence)

Tamil is the **launch language**, not a **universal pillar**. Pillars must survive language changes. This separates CORE identity from LOCALIZATION delivery — architecturally cleaner and expansion-friendly.

Lawrence also named the missing pillar: **Hope, Faith, Love of Jesus** — scripturally anchored in 1 Corinthians 13:13. This is the universal experiential fruit of Jesus's words (peace, trust, courage, love) — antidote to the world's chaos, negativity, and false teaching.

### Four Universal Pillars (never change regardless of language)

| # | Pillar | Answers |
|---|---|---|
| 📖 1 | **THE WORD** | What do I encounter? → Scripture, Words of Jesus, no paraphrasing, verse attribution |
| 🚶 2 | **THE WALK** | How do I live? → Mind-Body-Soul daily practice (arokia meaning) |
| 🕊️ 3 | **HOPE · FAITH · LOVE** | What grows in me? → Theological virtues, fruit of His words, antidote to chaos |
| 🛡️ 4 | **INTEGRITY** | Why can I trust this? → Return to roots, no noise, no false teachers, transparency, tool-not-Jesus |

### Layers on Top (expand over time)

- 🌍 **Language Layer** — v1: Tamil → Hindi → Telugu → Malayalam → Kannada → Bengali → Marathi → English
- 🎨 **Cultural Layer** — Tamil Christian heritage (keerthanaigal, Kaalai/Maalai, dialects, Velankanni) → expands per language
- 👥 **Audience Layer** — v1: 1-2 audiences → expand to kids/teens/elders/migrants/pastors
- ⛪ **Liturgical Layer** — v1: daily rhythm + 1 major season → full calendar over time
- 🤝 **Community Layer** — v1: advisory board visible only → pastor portal, testimony, contribs after user base exists

### Architectural Rules for Tamil-First Without Tamil-Locked

1. Content schema stores language as a swappable field — nothing hard-codes Tamil.
2. All copy lives in i18n translation files (React Native i18n standard).
3. Voice library is per-language ("Tamil Shepherd's Voice," later "Hindi Shepherd's Voice").
4. Cultural meditations are a first-class category but tagged by tradition.
5. Brand name "Arokia" stays (pan-Indian Sanskrit root already present).
6. Ecumenical + pan-Indian positioning visible on About page from v1.

### Seven Original Clusters Map to New Architecture

The 7 thematic clusters from ideation now map onto the new architecture:

- 📖 **The Word cluster** → becomes **Pillar 1: THE WORD**
- 🧠💪🕊️ **Practice cluster** → becomes **Pillar 2: THE WALK**
- 🛡️ **Integrity cluster** → becomes **Pillar 4: INTEGRITY** (plus contributes to Pillar 3 via "peace/hope" framing)
- *NEW* **Pillar 3: HOPE · FAITH · LOVE** — draws across clusters (Anxiety Library, Marriage Meditation, Forgiveness Journey, "Do Not Be Anxious," Walk with Jesus, Fruit of the Spirit) — experiential outcomes
- ஆ **Tamil Heart cluster** → becomes **Cultural & Language LAYER** (not a pillar)
- 👥 **The People cluster** → becomes **Audience LAYER**
- ⛪ **Liturgical Rhythm cluster** → becomes **Liturgical LAYER**
- 🤝 **The Body cluster** → becomes **Community LAYER**

### Theological Notes (from SCAMPER discussion)

- **No Sabbath Lock** (rejected): Jesus reframed Sabbath — "Sabbath was made for man" (Mark 2:27). Arokia follows Jesus's teaching, not Mosaic Sabbath law. Church-visit tracker is gentle encouragement only; no app-imposed Sabbath behavior.
- **Ecumenical** — serves all Christians (Catholic, Protestant, Pentecostal, Orthodox). Arokia Matha heritage honored without sectarian framing.
- **Jesus as the only source of truth** — the app's decision filter on all theological choices.

### Positioning Statement (draft)

> **Arokia** is a Tamil-first Christian app for wholeness — in mind, body, and soul — through the authentic words of Jesus. In a noisy age of false teachers and engagement-farming, Arokia is a quiet tool that returns you to Scripture, not to itself.

## Phase 4 Results — 60-Day MVP Plan (Resource Constraints)

### Constraints (confirmed by Lawrence)

- **Time:** 10-20 hrs/week
- **Budget:** ₹10-20k/month (start minimal; open to sponsors/donations to scale)
- **Timeline:** 60 days target — but with "foundation-first, robust, scalable" ethos; quality over rush
- **Voice:** Start with ElevenLabs AI Tamil voice; family member (female, Tamil) + community volunteers for v1.1+
- **Mindset:** "Not a hobby project — using technical skill to spread the words of Jesus."

### MVP Scope (v1.0 — ships by Day 60-75)

**The Word (minimal):** 50 Words-of-Jesus quotes (red-letter), Tamil audio + text + reference. Search. Bible-first hand-off.
**The Walk (minimal):** Triune home screen. 15 meditations (5 × Mind/Body/Soul). Silence Between Words mode.
**Hope·Faith·Love (minimal):** 5 anxiety-types → scripture-based meditations.
**Integrity:** Opening Vow, About page, Glass-Wall Budget (static), Pay-It-Forward 10%. Zero ads, zero notifications.
**Tamil Layer:** All content in Tamil + architecture ready for Hindi/Telugu (i18n + content schema language-agnostic).
**Donations:** Razorpay integration (UPI + cards), one-time + recurring, automated receipts, 10% forward visible.

### Deferred to v1.1 (3-6 months post-launch)

- Bedtime Jesus Stories for Kids (Lawrence's favorite #43)
- Family member voice records signature content (migrate from AI)
- WhatsApp Voice Bot for grandmothers
- Holy Week Journey (first major liturgical season)
- "Do Not Be Anxious" 21-day course (full)
- Print-to-Paper PDF export
- Community voice contributions

### Deferred to v2+ (6-12 months)

- Hindi language layer (only after Tamil product-market fit)
- Pastor Portal
- Testimony Archive
- Open Theology Layer
- Full audience modes (teens, elders, migrants, pastors)
- Counselor Referral

### Explicitly NEVER

- Celebrity pastor endorsements
- Engagement streaks for self-practice
- AI-Jesus personas
- Prosperity gospel content
- Push notification spam
- Subscription paywalls
- Sabbath Lock (rejected per Mark 2:27 — Jesus's teaching)

### Tech Stack

| Layer | Choice | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Framework | React Native + Expo (EAS) | Cross-platform, OTA updates |
| Language | TypeScript strict | Long-term maintainability |
| State | Zustand | Minimal, scalable |
| Styling | NativeWind (Tailwind) | Consistent, fast |
| i18n | react-i18next | Day-1 Tamil + ready for Hindi/Telugu |
| Backend | Supabase (free tier) | Postgres + auth + storage |
| Audio | react-native-track-player | Background + lockscreen |
| AI Voice | ElevenLabs Multilingual v2 (Tamil) | Generate → cache → own files |
| Payments | Razorpay | India-native: UPI, cards, netbanking |
| Scripture | Tamil OV Bible (public domain) | Free, authoritative |
| Analytics | PostHog (self-host) or minimal | Privacy-respecting, never Google Analytics |
| Errors | Sentry (free tier) | Early issue detection |

### Cost Projection

**One-time (during 60-day build):** ~₹12-18k (ElevenLabs intensive content gen + app store fees + domain).
**Ongoing monthly (post-launch):** < ₹1-2k/month (free tiers handle initial scale).
**Well within the ₹10-20k/month ceiling.**

### 60-Day Roadmap

- **Weeks 1-2 — Foundation** (~25 hrs): Project scaffold, i18n, content schema, Supabase setup, Tamil Bible data import, CI/CD.
- **Weeks 3-4 — The Word** (~25 hrs): 50 Jesus quotes, ElevenLabs Tamil audio, Red-Letter browser + search, verse attribution pattern.
- **Weeks 5-6 — The Walk + Hope/Faith/Love** (~30 hrs): Triune home screen, audio player, Silence Between Words mode, 15 meditations, 5 anxiety-type meditations.
- **Week 7 — Integrity + Donations** (~20 hrs): Opening Vow, About page, Glass-Wall Budget, Razorpay integration, Pay-It-Forward allocation.
- **Week 8 — Beta + Polish** (~15 hrs): 10-20 beta testers (family + church friends), bug fixes, accessibility.
- **Weeks 9-10 (buffer) — Launch** (~15 hrs): App Store + Google Play submissions, landing page, soft launch, first pay-forward.

**Total:** ~130 hours at 15 hrs/week average. Achievable in 60 days with focus; 75-day buffer for quality.

### Voice Strategy Evolution

- **v1.0:** ElevenLabs Tamil, honestly labeled as "AI-narrated."
- **v1.1:** Family member records signature content (Opening Vow, flagship meditations). AI continues for variety.
- **v2+:** Community volunteers (male + female, multiple dialects) for audience-specific modes.

**Honest framing:** The gradual AI → human voice migration becomes part of Arokia's story — community-voiced growth.
